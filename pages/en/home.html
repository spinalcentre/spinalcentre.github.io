peripheral nervous system (Neurosurgical center)

A neurosurgical center conducts consultations and treatment at different diseases and consequences of traumas of the central and peripheral nervous system, including degenerative diseases of spine, requiring the specialized treatment or rehabilitation.

Pain in the lumbar spine can be caused by a number of reasons. One of them is a hernia of intervertebral disk. People suppose often, that pain of back is caused by disk hernia, however it not always correctly, possible reason of pain is stenosis of spinal channel, spondylolistesis, pathological fraktura of vertebra, et cetera.

Disk hernia is one of the most dangerous and difficult diseases of spine. Reason of origin of disk hernia can be the most different factors and terms: wrong carriage, excessive loadings on a rachis, traumas, lack of development of muscule corset. On appearance of this difficult ailment can influence the age-related changes. Most often disk hernia is complication of osteochondrosis of spine.

Disk hernia causes problems two by constituents. Going out in a spinal channel a pulpose besides the mechanical affecting nervous structures causes their chemical irritation the matters contained in him. Combination of these factors results in a pain syndrome, weakness, numbness of that part of body, which is innervated by this nerve.
